Our Story

Patna's Iconic South Indian Restaurant

Since 1985, Bansi Vihar has been serving authentic South Indian cuisine to the people of Patna. What started as a small eatery has grown into one of the city's most beloved restaurants, known for its commitment to quality, authenticity, and taste.

We take pride in using traditional recipes passed down through generations, combined with the freshest ingredients to create dishes that transport you to the heart of South India. Our restaurant has had the honor of hosting notable guests, including the Chief Minister of Bihar, and continues to be a favorite among locals and visitors alike.
